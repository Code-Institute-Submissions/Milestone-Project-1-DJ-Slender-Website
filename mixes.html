Bringing The Music To Life


DJ Slender (also known as Slendz) is without a doubt regarded as being one of the best mix DJ's in the uk. Saluted by the industry for his quick mixes and his great understanding of music, because of that he is regarded as a RnB Slow Jam connoisseur!

About Slendz


A charismatic, humble, DJ with an ear for music. Slender is one of few DJ's that can turn a party over. Mixing the best in Club Classics (80's/90's/00's) Soul and Rare Groove with the ability to play many other genres such as Afrobeats, Grime, and Jungle.
